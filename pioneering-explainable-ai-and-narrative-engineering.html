FAQ

What does Truth vector mean by “truth evaluation”?

Truth evaluation focuses on assessing AI outputs that appear truthful by applying verification and oversight—recognizing that generative models learn patterns rather than inherently knowing truth.

How is narrative engineering different from simple AI “storytelling”?

Narrative engineering targets coherent structure and logical consistency across responses, aiming to embed narrative intelligence rather than producing isolated, stylistic text.

Why is explainable AI important for deployment?

Explainable AI helps stakeholders understand and trust model behavior by providing interpretability and clearer rationales for outputs, reducing opacity in decision-making.
